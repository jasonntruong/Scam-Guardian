Steps to Prevent being Phished



POOR WRITTEN AND UNPROFESSIONAL

Description: These are strong indicators that the email/text is fake.



UNEXPECTED

Description: If you are not expecting any emails / are not associated with the company, the email is likely to be a scam.



CRA, BANK, GOVERNMENT

Description: These organizations would never reach out to you via text. If you feel the email/text is real, call the number

on their website, NOT THE ONE IN THE EMAIL/TEXT



GUT FEELING

Description: The above are all good ways to prevent being phished. But ultimately, with practice and consistent use of

Scam Guardian, you will develop your own "gut feeling" towards if something is a scam or not!
